## System Dependencies

The scripts install the following packages from the distribution's official `apt` repositories: `git`, `sassc`, `gnome-tweaks`, `gnome-shell-extensions`, `gnome-shell-extension-manager`, `libglib2.0-dev-bin`, `imagemagick`, `dialog`, `curl`, `unzip`. These are standard open-source packages available under their respective licenses (typically GPL-2.0 or GPL-3.0).
